dpfifo -> dual clock, read: 64 bits, write: 16 bits, normal fifo (não é show ahead), sem uso dos signals "used";

dpfifo_2 -> dual clock, read: 64 bits, write: 16 bits, normal fifo (não é show ahead), com uso dos signals "used";

dpfifo_3 -> dual clock, read: 64 bits, write: 16 bits, show ahead fifo, com uso dos signals "used";
